Why is 88 Vintage Important?

88 Vintage is committed to making sustainable fashion attainable. We recognize that many sustainable brands are relatively expensive. Although we provide you with updated lists of the hottest brands, we also present cheaper alternatives as well as custom options that you can create in the comfort of your home. We are dedicated to shedding light on the negative impacts fast fashion has on both our health & environment. Whether it's increasing the carbon foot print, water consumption & pollution or unfair labor practices, the irreversible harms are rapidly growing alongside of our consumption rate. If we as consumers become more conscious of our spending habits & buying power, we can make a significant change in the right direction. 88 Vintage hopes to be a tool that allows you all to make conscious and educated decisions when it comes to fashion.
