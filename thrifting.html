Thrifting 101

What's the Difference?

Chain Stores

Chain thrift stores are a great place to start because many of them have deals with local stores to obtain new items and sell them for a lower price. Chain thrift stores have fast turnover, so if you keep going, chances are you will always find something good.

Antique/Vintage Shops

Vintage or antique stores can be less overwhelming than an average thrift store because someone has already sifted through the junk. The price will reflect both the original purchase price as well as the trouble the purchaser has gone through to find the piece.

Consignment Shops

Consignment stores are slightly different from thrift stores in that they do not own their inventory. Stores acts as a middleman between the seller and the purchaser, and takes a cut of the profit when the item sells. Prices in consignment stores tend to be higher, but the quality tends to be better.
